Privacy Policy – Filanto Store

Last updated: 29 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Filanto Store (a footwear shopping app) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account & contact data: name, phone number, email address.
  • Order data: purchased items, order details, order history.
  • Delivery data: shipping address and details required to fulfill delivery.
  • Usage & diagnostics: app performance data and crash logs to improve stability.

2) How We Use Information

  • Process orders, payments, delivery, and customer support.
  • Communicate with you about order status or support requests.
  • Improve user experience and app performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations when required.

3) Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service and for operational/legal purposes. As a general rule:

  • Orders & invoices: up to 24 months (or longer if required by law).
  • Support tickets & technical logs: up to 12 months.

4) Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data only when necessary:

  • Service providers to fulfill the service (e.g., shipping, payment providers) only as needed.
  • Legal compliance or official requests when required.

5) Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to measure performance and improve the app, such as:

  • Google Firebase (e.g., Crashlytics / Analytics) to analyze crashes and performance.
  • Google Analytics (if enabled) to understand app usage.

These services may collect technical/statistical information under their own policies.

6) Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child provided data, please contact us to request deletion.

7) Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements).
  • Object to certain processing where applicable.

8) Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data; however, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

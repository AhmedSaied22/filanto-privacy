Privacy Policy – Filanto

Last Updated: 29 January 2026

Filanto is committed to protecting users’ privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Filanto application.

1) Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address
  • Order and purchase information
  • App usage and technical data for improvement purposes

2) How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process orders and deliveries
  • Communicate with users regarding orders and customer support
  • Improve user experience and service quality
  • Operate and manage the application securely and efficiently
  • Comply with legal obligations when required

3) Payment Methods

Filanto provides the following payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery
  • Bank Transfer

No external online payment gateways are used within the app.

4) Data Sharing

We do not sell user data or share it with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share limited information only in the following cases:

  • Order fulfillment (e.g., logistics and delivery services)
  • Legal compliance or official requests
  • Business merger, partnership, sale, or ownership transfer

5) Data Retention

We retain data only as necessary for service and legal purposes. As a general guideline:

  • Order data: up to 24 months
  • Technical and operational data: up to 12 months

Longer retention may apply if required by law or to resolve disputes.

6) Third-Party Services

The app may use technical services for:

  • Performance monitoring
  • Crash analysis
  • Usage analytics

These services are used for technical improvement purposes only.

7) Children’s Privacy

Filanto is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us to request deletion.

8) User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Request access, updates, or corrections to their data
  • Request deletion of their data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Know how their data is used

9) Data Security

Filanto applies appropriate security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10) Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
